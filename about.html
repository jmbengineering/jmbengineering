JMB Engineering was established in 2015 the goal of becoming a major international manufacturer of high quality submersible pumps that would meet or exceed international quality standards, be competitively priced, and would be supported by an organization that would provide excellent service to our customers, while at the same time considering the impact of our pumps and activities on the environment. By constantly and diligently working with these goals in mind, Stairs Industrial Co. Ltd. has grown to be a major international pump manufacturer. .
