What We Do

Steel Security Gates

They have a robust double trellis design that opens and closes smoothly and easily. Customers can choose between steel or aluminium sliding gates. There is a choice of either the Trellidor patented deadlock or slamlock. These expanding gates can be custom-made to fit doors, windows, passages, patios, hatches and most other building openings, keeping them safe from intrusion.

Steel Balustrates

Ideal for sweeping staircases and top-floor balconies, a horizontal balustrade system will enhance the architectural features of any home. EZ-Rails Ready-Made Horizontal Kit comprises a 50.8 top rail with 50.8 main posts; 38.1 centre posts and 15.8 horizontal cross tubes. Safety is ensured as the in-fills are placed slightly off-set.
